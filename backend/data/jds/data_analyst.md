# Data Analyst

We are looking for a Data Analyst who can convert business questions into clear
analysis, dashboards, and actionable recommendations. The analyst should be able
to work with messy data, explain insights to stakeholders, and maintain reliable
reporting workflows.

Key responsibilities:
- Query and analyze data using SQL.
- Build dashboards and reports using Power BI, Tableau, Looker, or similar
  tools.
- Clean, validate, and transform data from multiple sources.
- Define metrics, investigate trends, and communicate findings clearly.
- Partner with business teams to support planning and decision-making.

Required skills:
- Strong SQL and spreadsheet skills.
- Experience with data visualization and dashboard design.
- Ability to reason about metrics, data quality, and business context.
- Clear communication and stakeholder management.

Good to have:
- Python experience with pandas or notebooks.
- Exposure to data warehouses such as BigQuery, Snowflake, Redshift, or
  PostgreSQL.
- Basic statistics or forecasting experience.
